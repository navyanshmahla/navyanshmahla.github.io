Hola amigos! Myself Navyansh Mahla, currently a pre-final year student at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT-Bombay). My academic pursuits are centered around exploring the vast expanse of computer science and artificial intelligence, fueled by my involvement with the prestigious Koita Centre of Digital Health (KCDH) at IIT-Bombay, under the guidance of eminent professors such as Prof. Kshitij Jadhav and Prof. Ganesh Ramakrishnan.

In particular, my research interests lie predominantly within the domain of foundational models, specifically concerning language and vision models built upon transformer architectures. These cutting-edge models pique my curiosity due to their “black box” nature, driving my eagerness to explore avenues for enhancing their inherent logical and reasoning capabilities.

Throughout my academic career, I have committed substantial efforts towards tackling pressing concerns in machine learning within federalized frameworks. In light of growing emphasis on user privacy, I aspire to dedicate further resources to advancing the frontiers of private and secure machine learning practices. Specifically, I aim to contribute significantly to the field of federated learning optimization – an area I am actively exploring alongside Amazon India. I am open to explore newer horizons in the field of machine learning and research on newer ideas.

At IIT-Bombay, I have wholeheartedly engaged in numerous extracurricular activities, complementing my rigorous academic curriculum. Throughout my sophomore year, I served as the Convener for the Web and Coding Club (WnCC), where I played a pivotal role in orchestrating a variety of initiatives, including:

  • Organizing several events, workshops, and hackathons designed to foster creativity, innovation, and skill development among fellow students.
  • Built the engine of CodeWars V3, the third iteration of India’s first bot programming contest organised by WnCC

Presently, I hold the position of Founding Manager for the burgeoning Artificial Intelligence community at IIT-Bombay. Spearheading this initiative, our team successfully developed InstiGPT, a pioneering educational institution-based chatbot heralding a new era in interactive knowledge dissemination. This project marks a significant milestone as the world’s first of its kind, demonstrating our unwavering commitment to leveraging technology for enhanced learning experiences.
